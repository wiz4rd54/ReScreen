ReScreen

Machine-based Resume Screening

ABOUT

ReScreen is a powerful tool that helps recruiters and employers to quickly and accurately evaluate job applicants' resumes. The tool works by scanning the CV for relevant information and then analyzing it against pre-defined criteria.

One of the key benefits of ReScreen is that it saves recruiters and employers a significant amount of time by automating the screening process. Rather than manually reviewing each CV, which can be time-consuming and often subjective, the tool helps to prioritize the most suitable candidates based on their qualifications and experience.

In addition to saving time, ReScreen also helps to reduce bias in the hiring process. By using objective criteria to evaluate candidates, the tool ensures that all applicants are given a fair and equal chance to be considered for the role.

Another benefit of using ReScreen is that it can identify gaps in a candidate's experience or qualifications. For example, if the job requires a specific skill or qualification that is not mentioned in the candidate's CV, the tool will flag this as an area of concern.

Overall, ReScreen is a valuable tool for both recruiters and employers. By automating the screening process and reducing bias, it helps to identify the most suitable candidates for the role and ensure that the hiring process is efficient and fair.

HOW IT WORKS

The recruiter will upload the resumes ( in .pdf or .docx ) on the portal under a specific job role.

The recruiter will add the job description. Based on the job description the model will rate the resumes.

Once the resumes are rated, the recruiter will be able to see the best resumes and will be able to shortlist the candidates.
